Billing

When am I charged?

Never during the trial. Your card is saved by Stripe when you start, and the first charge happens only after the full 7 days. Cancel before then and you pay nothing.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes. Upgrades apply immediately with prorated billing; downgrades apply at your next renewal. Just reply to any receipt email or use the account page.

Is my card information safe?

Payment happens entirely on Stripe, the processor used by millions of businesses. Your card number is never seen by, or stored on, ScoreQuest.
