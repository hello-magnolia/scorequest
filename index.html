How your quest works

A path, not a pile of PDFs. Three loops carry you from first login to test day.

  1. 01

    Take the diagnostic quest

    Twenty-five minutes, adaptive, zero stakes. It maps all eight domains onto your skill tree — every weak node becomes a marked quest on your world map.

  2. 02

    Clear daily side quests

    Fifteen-minute question sets tuned to yesterday's mistakes. Keep the streak alive, bank XP, and unlock gear for your avatar.

  3. 03

    Fight the boss battles

    Full-length, fully timed mock exams in the official digital format. Every boss you clear sharpens your score forecast.
